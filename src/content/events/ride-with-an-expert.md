Learn More Your Custom Gravel Adventure Not every gravel journey needs to be an ultra-endurance, unsupported race. Sometimes, you want a bit of comfort—a structured tour with the right balance of adventure and ease. If you'd prefer a softer landing, a follow car for support, and the comfort of knowing you'll end each day with a hot shower and a hearty dinner at a hotel, we've got you covered. While our passion lies in the raw experience of unsupported bikepacking, we understand that not everyone wants to rough it all the time. That's why we're offering customized gravel tours tailored to your needs. You set the dates and the budget, and we'll handle the rest. We'll plan the perfect route, book the accommodations, and provide expert guidance on the road to ensure you get the most out of your gravel adventure. What We Offer: Customizable Itineraries : Tailored routes that match your skill level and interests, showcasing the stunning landscapes of South Africa. Support Vehicle : A follow car to carry your gear, provide refreshments, and offer assistance along the way. Accommodation &amp; Meals : Pre-booked lodging and dining options to suit your preferences, from rustic lodges to luxury stays. Expert Guidance : Ride with an experienced gravel cyclist who will guide you through the journey, offering tips and local insights. Our goal is to introduce you to the beauty and thrill of gravel cycling in a way that's accessible and enjoyable, whether you're new to the sport or looking to explore without the hassle of planning.
