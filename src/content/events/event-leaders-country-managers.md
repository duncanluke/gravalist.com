Event Leaders Robbie Ferri Robbie Ferri is a seasoned Ultra cyclist from the UK. He has been ultra cycling for around a decade and been in many races worldwide. He has also broken world records and trained many athletes to ultra cycle in his time in the industry. Living in Norfolk and training on this terrain day after day makes him a local expert of the area. The route takes you through some of his favourite places in the region and through a journey of the most iconic places Norfolk has to offer.
