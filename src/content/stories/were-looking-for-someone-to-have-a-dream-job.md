**Location: Flexible, Preference for USA, UK, or SA**

**Job Type: Part-Time**

**About The Gravalist Company:**

The Gravalist Company is a dynamic and innovative organization specializing in organizing ultra-distance backpacking races that push the limits of endurance and adventure. We are dedicated to creating transformative experiences for participants while fostering a strong sense of community. Our events take cyclists through breathtaking landscapes, historical cities, and personal journeys of self-discovery.

**Job Description:**

We are seeking a passionate and skilled Ultra Distance Cyclist with a flair for event management, photography, and a touch of philosophy or psychology. The ideal candidate will play a crucial role in designing, implementing, and managing our ultra-distance backpacking races, ensuring an unforgettable experience for participants.

**Responsibilities:**

1.  **Route Design and Implementation:**
    
    -   Develop and design challenging yet scenic routes for ultra-distance backpacking races.
    -   Collaborate with local authorities and communities to obtain necessary permits for events.
2.  **City Highlights and Photography:**
    
    -   Explore and highlight the historical and cultural aspects of cities along the race routes.
    -   Capture high-quality photographs of key locations and memorable moments during riders' journeys.
3.  **CRM Tools and Website, Komoot:**
    
    -   Fully manage and optimize CRM tools, the company website, and utilize Komoot for route planning and navigation.
    -   Ensure seamless registration, tracking, and communication throughout the event.
4.  **Psychological Support Journey:**
    
    -   Design and implement a psychological support program for participants, offering guidance and support throughout their journey.
    -   Collaborate with experts in psychology to enhance the mental well-being of participants.
5.  **Event Day Management:**
    
    -   Oversee event logistics on race days, ensuring a smooth and safe experience for participants.
    -   Coordinate with support staff, volunteers, and vendors to execute successful events.
6.  **Performance Reporting:**
    
    -   Provide regular reports to the management team on key performance criteria, including participant feedback, event success metrics, and areas for improvement.

**Requirements:**

-   Extensive experience in ultra-distance cycling.
-   Strong organizational and project management skills.
-   Proficient in event management, CRM tools, website management, and Komoot.
-   Excellent photography skills.
-   Basic knowledge of psychology or philosophy.
-   Ability to travel frequently for route scouting and event management.
-   Exceptional communication and interpersonal skills.

**How to Apply:**

If you are an adventurous spirit with a love for cycling, event management, and a keen eye for capturing moments, we invite you to join our team. Please submit your resume, a cover letter, and a portfolio of your photography work to [hello@gravalist.com](../../../com/gravalist/index.html) with the subject line "Dream Job Application - \[Your Name\]".
