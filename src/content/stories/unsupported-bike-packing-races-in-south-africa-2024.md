## Event Categories

The Unsupported Bike Packing Races encompass two distinct categories, ensuring a diverse range of experiences for participants. The solo category, with five available slots, offers individual cyclists the chance to test their mettle against rugged terrains and unpredictable elements. Simultaneously, the touring category accommodates eight entries for pairs and tourers, fostering camaraderie and shared experiences amidst the challenging journey.
